Group Activity Week 12: Stories to Remember, part 1

4 4 unread replies. 5 5 replies.

Purpose
This brief activity is a chance to think back and share something positive as we near our time to say "farewell."    

Years from now when you're older, younger people may ask you what it was like to go to college during this time. What is one specific story that you might share? Pause for a moment, take a deep breath, and reflect on something from this past semester that will be memorable to you.  It doesn't have to be something grand or earth-shattering.  Then just take one minute to share the story of something you will always remember from this semester.  Maybe something you're grateful for; maybe something funny; maybe a bit of wisdom you'd like to pass on to others--anything that stands out for you from this semester. 

Task, Part 1

Please share your story in a one-minute video.  You can use the "Upload/Record Media" in the Canvas tool bar above if your device has a built in camera and mic, and simply record from your device if that works for you.  Or you can record your video and load it using "My Media" in Canvas (use the little multi-colored icon in the tool bar above).  Call LTS 715836-5711 for help if you need it.

Task, Part 2
Some time before November 29th, return to this Discussion page and view everyone's short videos. Then choose any three videos and post a brief, friendly response. You can write whatever you like, upload an image, or even record a video in response. You will earn full credit as long as your response is specific enough to show that you watched your classmate's video. Let's work together to try to distribute our responses so that everyone gets a couple of comments on their video. 

Grading
You will receive 5 points for your story and 5 points for posting 3 responses.
