Fish Tickets:
$25, one ticket required for each fish registered, multiple tickets may be purchased

  • Over $80,000 in cash and prizes including a Ford F150 Truck!
  • Prizes awarded to the top 25 fish and every 5th place thereafter by weight to the 300th place so even a small fish can win big!
  • The winner of the 2-year lease from Eau Claire Ford for the 2019 Ford F-150 will be drawn at random from the tickets stubs of all registered fish.
  • Everyone who registers a fish may request two free Blugold Football Tickets at the weigh-in stations (value $16).
  • $100 gift cards will be given to the top fish by species: Northern, Walleye and Pan Fish.
  • “The One that Got Away” drawing from all Fish Ticket holders that did not register a fish. Turn in Fish Ticket stub at the Prize Table at The View by 2:30pm.

Fish Ticket Availability:
Tickets will be available online until 10:00pm on Friday, February 8th. Fish Tickets will be available on February 9th, 2019 at the weigh-in stations and The View until 9am for $40.

  • Fish Tickets purchased online will be mailed through February 1st to the address indicated. After February 1st, tickets will be available for pick-up at the weigh-in station at The View. NOTE: You do not need to pick up your Fish Tickets until you are ready to weigh in a fish.

Raffle Tickets:
$5 each or 5 for $20

  • $7,500 in prizes includes a 2019 Polaris Sportsman 450 4-wheeler! Tickets can be purchased up until the drawing on Saturday, February 9th at the weigh-in stations and The View.

  • Raffle tickets purchased online will have the stubs completed by contest officials and entered into the drawing. Claim tickets will be mailed through February 1st to the address indicated. After February 1st, claim tickets will be available for pick-up at the weigh-in station at The View. NOTE: Raffle drawing begins at 3:00pm at The View. Please pick up claim tickets by 2:00pm. Need not be present to win.
