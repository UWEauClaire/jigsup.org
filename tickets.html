Fish Tickets:
$25, one ticket required for each fish registered, multiple tickets may be purchased

  • Over $80,000 in cash and prizes including a 2-year lease on a New Ford F-150!
  • Prizes awarded by weight to the top 20 fish and every 10th place thereafter to 480th place, then 481st through 500th.
  • The winner of the 2-year lease from Eau Claire Ford for the Ford F-150 will be drawn at random from the tickets stubs of all registered fish.
  • $100 gift cards will be given to the top fish by species: Northern, Walleye and Pan Fish.
  • “The One that Got Away” drawing from all Fish Ticket holders that did not register a fish. Turn in Fish Ticket stub at The View-by 2:30pm.

Fish Ticket Availability:
Tickets will be available online until 10:00pm on Friday, February 11th. Fish Tickets will be available on February 12th, 2022 at the weigh-in stations and The View 7-9am for $40.

  • Fish Tickets purchased online will be mailed through February 4th to the address indicated. After February 4th, tickets will be available for pick-up at the weigh-in station at The View. Tickets must be picked up at the Weigh-In Station at The View by 2pm. NOTE: You do not need to pick up your Fish Tickets until you are ready to weigh in a fish.
