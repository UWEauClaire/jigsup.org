Prizes are the responsibility of the winners, including registration and taxes. Prizes valued at over $500 will require the social security number of the winner. Proper identification is required from all winners in order to complete the 1099-MISC form in accordance with State of Wisconsin tax law, “FEDERAL AND STATE WITHHOLDING ON PAYMENTS TO INDIVIDUALS “ which can be found at http://www.bussvc.wisc.edu/acct/policy/tax/taxapp2.html.
