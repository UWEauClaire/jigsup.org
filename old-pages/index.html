UWEC Recreation, Blugold Athletics, and the Lake Wissota Lions Club presents:

6th Annual JIG'S UP Blugold Ice Fishing Contest

Date:

Saturday, February 9th, 2019



Time:

7am-2pm, no pre-registration



Site:

Weigh-In Stations: The Edge Pub & Eatery and The View on Lake Wissota
Prize Distribution: The View on Lake Wissota starting at 3pm

Prize Information:

Over $80,000 in cash and prizes including a 2-year lease on a 2019 Ford F150 Truck drawn from all registered Fish Tickets! Prizes awarded by weight: top 25 fish and every 5th place thereafter to 300th place so even a small fish can win big! Each participant may register one fish per ticket; tickets are $25 with no limit on the number of tickets an individual can purchase. Raffle prizes include a 2019 Polaris Sportsman 450 4-wheeler, 4-$500 Scheels Gift Cards, $100 Mega! CO-OP Gift Cards, Steak Packages, and more! Raffle tickets are $5 each or 5 for $20! $100 Gift Cards are awarded to the top fish by weight in each category: northern, walleye, and pan fish.

All state regulations and bag limits apply and no person may bring in more than the legal limit of fish allowed, which for this contest includes northern, walleye, and pan fish.

Purchase tickets at the following locations:

  • Online at jigsup.org
  • Scheels (bait available)
  • Eau Claire Ford
  • Select Mega Holiday Stations
    • Raffle tickets not sold at these locations
  • Jacobson's Hardware (bait available)
  • UWEC Recreation
  • The SandBar and Grill
  • The View at Lake Wissota
  • The Edge Pub & Eatery on Lake Wissota
For contest rules and additional information, visit jigsup.org or contact UWEC Recreation at (715)836-3377.
